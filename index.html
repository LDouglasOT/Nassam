Learn About Us

Uganda Based non-profit Islamic charity organization

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Our Islamic charity organization is dedicated to serving the global Muslim community and promoting the values of Islam. We believe in the power of charitable giving and compassionate action to make a positive impact on the world. With a team of dedicated volunteers and a commitment to excellence, we strive to provide essential resources and support for those in need. Whether it's through financial assistance, educational programs, or community outreach, we are committed to making a difference in the lives of others. If you share our passion for service and are looking for a way to get involved, we invite you to join us in making a positive difference in the world.
"Our mission is to serve the global Muslim community by providing essential resources and support for those in need. Through the power of charitable giving and compassionate action, we strive to make a positive impact on the world and promote the values of Islam."
"Our vision is to serve as a beacon of compassion and justice, guided by the principles of Islam and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). We strive to alleviate suffering, promote social harmony, and empower marginalized communities around the world, through innovative and sustainable programs that enhance human dignity and well-being. We envision a world where every person has the opportunity to fulfill their potential and live a life of purpose and fulfillment, regardless of their circumstances."

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lives and promote islamic values in the african community with you

Healthy Food

We help feed health food to the homeless through funds from our donators accross the globe

Pure Water

We dig and construct water pumps for the different communities in order to promote health living in the arican society

Health Care

We do immunisation, vaccination, and provide health services to the needy wwho can't afford them, thanks to your donations

Islamic Education

We teach the Quran and the Islamic values to the african society helping to get the deen know deep in the african villages

Social Care

We train health workers who then work locally in their villages to render services and give a caring and essential services to the needy,you donations make this possible
